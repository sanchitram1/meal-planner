## Ingredients

- 250g of Ginger and Garlic
- 3 tbsp of oil
- Salt to taste

## Directions

- Chop and rinse thoroughly
- Blend! Smooth consistency, i.e., no chunks

## Notes

```ad-attention
This lasts for about 1 month, and must be refrigerated
```
