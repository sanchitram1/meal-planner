## Ingredients
- 1 cup oats
- 2 cups milk
- 1 tablespoon honey
- 1/2 cup mixed berries

## Instructions
1. Mix oats and milk in a bowl.
2. Add honey and stir well.
3. Top with mixed berries.
4. Enjoy your breakfast!
